Macbook Repairs - MAY 2023

From a young age, the thought of owning a MacBook fueled my passion for technology. However, financial constraints posed a challenge. Undeterred, I devised a resourceful strategy to fulfill my dream. So, I started acquiring faulty MacBook’s through Facebook Marketplace. My goal was to refurbish these devices and make them fully operational for resale or personal use.

One notable achievement within this endeavor was the successful repair of a MacBook’s storage drive. The issue was identified as a faulty capacitor, which I skillfully resoldered, breathing new life into the computer and restoring it to a completely usable state.

  • Perfected soldering skills by replacing a dead capacitor
  • Learned to diagnose and isolate issues through deduction
  • Marketed refurbished macbooks

Digital clock

I created a multifunctional clock that displays date and time, incorporating features such as accounting for leap years adding an extra day to February. Additionally, I designed it so that the date and time with buttons. This project taught me how to use external I2C Liquid Crystal Displays by utilizing C++ libraries to accomplish my goals. Some experiance I gained are:

  • Developed problem-solving skills by addressing complexities such as leap years and ensuring accurate timekeeping, showcasing a logical and systematic approach to challenges in the project.
  • Learned to program and control an embedded system, integrating functionalities like date and time display using C++ libraries.
  • Acquired expertise in integrating external hardware components, specifically I2C Liquid Crystal Displays which helped me in future projects.
